ABOUT

Nice to meet you. My name is Kat Taylor, and I am currently a senior at Eastern Washington University. I'm 21 years old, born on Noveber 1, 1992 in Seattle. I've been drawing since I could hold a writing utensil, and when I got a little older I traded the pens and pencils for a Wacom tablet and never looked back. I began my digital painting experience in Photoshop, and during my time at EWU I've also become familiar with Illustrator and InDesign.

When I'm not drawing or writing, I'm listening to music, reading, or playing video games.

STUDIO ART MAJOR / VCD MINOR

Although I have an art background in digital painting first and foremost, I decided to minor in VCD because the art program emphasized fine art, not illustration. It took me a couple quarters to figure out what I wanted to do, but in the end I decided to continue to major in art because of my love of painting and drawing. I picked up the VCD minor to augment my abilities and I've loved every moment of it.

My goal is to someday work on video games as a concept artist, an illustrator, or in the comics industry as a penciler or colorist.
